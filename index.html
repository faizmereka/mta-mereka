About Mereka Talent Accelerator

The Mereka Talent Accelerator is a nationwide initiative committed to equipping Malaysia's youth with the essential skills and mindset needed to succeed in the future of work.

Powered by Yayasan Hasanah, in collaboration with Biji-biji Initiative and Mereka, the programme offers expert-led digital skills training, personalised career coaching, and ongoing community support.

Our Vision

Empowering youth to thrive in the digital economy through skills, mindset, and opportunities while driving career growth, economic resilience, and meaningful community impact.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality digital skills training and career development resources that empower young Malaysians to build sustainable careers in the digital economy.

Our Values

We believe in accessibility, inclusivity, innovation, and sustainable impact. Our program is designed to bridge gaps and create opportunities for all eager to learn.

Participant Commitment

To make the most of the Mereka Talent Accelerator Programme, participants are required to commit to the following phases:

Overall Commitment: 1½ Year

6 months of experiential-based learning.

8 months of empowerment, peer support, and career tracking.

Awareness Phase

  • Attend Pre-Learning Sessions (3 hours)
  • Join Webinars & On-ground Workshop

Upskilling Phase

  • Complete a 3-Day Physical Bootcamp (21 hours)
  • Finish Virtual Learning Modules (16 hours)

Employability Phase

  • Participate in Career Coaching Sessions (8 hours)
  • Attend Employer Networking Events
  • Create and Refine Your Career Portfolio

Community & Impact Phase

  • Join Monthly Peer Support Sessions
  • Complete Quarterly Income & Career Progress Surveys
  • Participate in Alumni Community Activities
